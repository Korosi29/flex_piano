Hi There!

Welcome to this beginner’s guide to playing the piano! Learning the piano may seem overwhelming at first, but with the right approach, anyone can do it. This guide is designed to make learning easy, even if you have never touched a piano before.

In this course, we will start with the basics—understanding the piano keys, octaves, and intervals. These are the building blocks of music and will help you make sense of everything else you learn. Each lesson is designed to be simple and practical, so you can start playing right away.
