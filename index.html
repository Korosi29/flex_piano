Welcome to Flex Piano!

Introduction

Are you a beginner or someone looking to refine your skills on the piano? Flex Piano is designed to guide you through the fundamentals in a simple, visual, and beginner-friendly way. Note! Learners will often discover that our lessons are at some point gospel-focused. That is because we are aimed at teaching gospel piano only in our sunsequent advanced courses.

Why Learn the Piano?

  • The piano is an excellent starting point for understanding music.
  • It enhances coordination, memory, and creativity.
  • It allows you to play a variety of musical styles, from classical to contemporary.

What You'll Discover Here

  • A visual guide to piano keys to help you get familiar
  • A visual guide to piano chords and how they work
  • How to identify notes and play simple melodies
  • How to use simple chord progressions to play some basic worship songs
  • Step-by-step exercises to build confidence
  • Practical tips to improve your technique
