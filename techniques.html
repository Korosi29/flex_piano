Beginner Piano Techniques

1. Finger Numbers

Each finger has a number: Thumb is 1, index is 2, middle is 3, ring is 4, and pinky is 5. Use these to stay organized.

2. Hand Position

Keep your hands curved like you're holding a ball. Don’t flatten your fingers or raise your wrists too high. Relax!

3. Play with Both Hands

Start with one hand. Later, try pressing the same note (like C) with both hands to build coordination.

4. Use the Black Keys

Group of 2 black keys? The white key before it is C. Group of 3? The white key before it is F. Use this to find your way around.

5. Practice Slowly

Don’t rush. Play slowly and clearly. Speed will come naturally with time and control.

6. Listen While You Play

Use your ears. If it sounds off, check your hand position. Your ears are your best tool!
