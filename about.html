About

This site was built to help absolute beginners learn the basics of playing the piano without needing to read sheet music or watch long videos.

We focus on simple, interactive lessons using visual piano keys. You can click the mini-piano icon at top-right to have your practical virtual piano you can click or tap. Whether you're using a real keyboard or just your phone or computer screen, you'll be able to follow along and start playing right away.

This site is lightweight, accessible, and completely free. It's built using just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure it works smoothly even on older devices.
